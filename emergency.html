Emergency Help

The Bahrain Cultural Office in Beijing may assist, within its scope, in following up on urgent or special cases affecting Bahraini students in China. This includes health, legal, administrative, humanitarian or other urgent matters requiring coordination or referral. If you are facing a serious issue, please follow the guidance below.

When to Seek Urgent Help

If your immediate health, safety, legal position or welfare is at risk, you should seek urgent assistance. Examples include serious illness or injury, legal issues, threats, loss of passport or documents, or other crises that cannot wait.

What to Send Immediately

When contacting the Cultural Office or the relevant authority, provide the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Passport number, if available
  • University and city
  • Your current location
  • A clear summary of the issue and immediate risks
  • Your contact number and preferred communication method
  • Name and contact details of a family member or emergency contact, if appropriate

Important Note

The Cultural Office provides support within its official scope. Certain urgent situations may require direct embassy involvement, university action, medical assistance or law enforcement. Use the most appropriate emergency channel based on your circumstances.
