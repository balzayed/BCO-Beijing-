Current Students

This section provides information for Bahraini students who are already enrolled in universities or academic programmes in China. The Cultural Office offers ongoing support with academic, administrative and procedural matters.

Update Your Record

If any of your personal or academic details change—such as your contact information, address, university, programme or study status—you must update your record with the Cultural Office to ensure that we can reach you and maintain accurate records.

Update Your Record

Academic Follow‑Up

The Office periodically reviews students’ academic status based on academic documents such as transcripts or proof of enrolment. If you require assistance regarding your academic progression or are facing difficulties, please contact us for guidance.

Request Academic Follow‑Up

Change of Major

If you wish to change your academic specialisation, you must submit a formal request along with supporting documents. The request will be reviewed based on academic and procedural considerations. A change of major is not automatic and may involve coordination with your university.

Submit Change of Major Request

Transfer Between Universities or Programmes

Students wishing to transfer to another university or programme must submit a request with evidence of new admission or transfer possibility, academic transcripts and an explanation of the reasons. The Office reviews each case individually.

Submit Transfer Request

Academic Warning & Special Requests

When facing academic difficulties or special circumstances, the Office can provide guidance and, where appropriate, coordinate with your institution. Contact us to discuss your situation and submit relevant documents for review.

Request Assistance

Official Letters and Administrative Requests

Students may request official letters, certificates or other administrative documents as needed. These requests must include the purpose of the letter, the receiving entity and any required supporting information.

Request an Official Letter
