Contact Us

For inquiries, service requests, document matters or student support issues, please use the contact channels below. Selecting the appropriate topic helps us respond to your enquiry more efficiently.

General Inquiries

For general questions regarding office services and procedures.

Email: info@bahrainculture.cn

Student Registration

For first‑time registration and opening your student file.

Email: register@bahrainculture.cn

Official Letters & Documents

For requests relating to letters, certificates, attestations and document matters.

Email: documents@bahrainculture.cn

Graduation & CHSI

For questions about graduation documentation, CHSI issues and follow‑up.

Email: graduation@bahrainculture.cn

Emergency & Urgent Matters

For urgent matters requiring immediate attention within the Office’s remit.

Phone: +86 123 456 7890 (24/7)

Embassy Contact

The Embassy handles consular, diplomatic and serious emergencies, such as lost passports or legal issues. Contact the Embassy of the Kingdom of Bahrain in Beijing through the appropriate channels in such cases.

Embassy Phone: +86 987 654 3210

When contacting the Office, please include your full name, university, programme, city and a clear summary of your request so that we can assist you promptly.
